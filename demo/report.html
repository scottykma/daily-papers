1. TetherCache: Stabilizing Autoregressive Long-Form Video Generation with Gated Recall and Trusted Alignment

  • Authors Yu Meng, Xiangyang Luo, Letian Li, Wenyuan Jiang, Chen Gao
  • Reason Directly addresses long video generation with memory/cache mechanisms
  • arXiv 2606.13035v1 · cs.CV, cs.AI
  • Note 17 pages, 8 figures
This paper proposes TetherCache, a training-free cache management strategy for stabilizing autoregressive long-form video generation. By introducing gated recall and trusted alignment mechanisms, it preserves diverse historical information within a fixed cache budget while reducing visual quality degradation from historical feature drift. Experiments on 30s to 240s videos show significant quality improvements.

2. OmniDirector: General Multi-Shot Camera Cloning without Cross-Paired Data

  • Authors Jiwen Liu, Shujuan Li, Zhixue Fang, Xiaohan Li, Yan Zhou
  • Reason Camera motion cloning for multi-shot video generation, core sub-topic
  • arXiv 2606.13432v1 · cs.CV, cs.AI
  • Note 12 pages, 8 figures
This paper introduces OmniDirector, a general framework for multi-shot camera motion cloning that encodes camera motion as grid motion videos, enabling director-level control without requiring cross-paired training data. The method scales to million-level datasets, harmonizes character, action, and camera control signals through a novel hierarchical prompt expansion agent.
